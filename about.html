About Segun Abon

Segun Abon is a gospel minister, worship leader, and spirit-filled composer devoted to the work of the Kingdom through music. With a passion for soul-lifting worship and prophetic praise, he continues to impact lives and bring hope to multitudes across churches and worship gatherings.

Ministry Through Music

His compositions are rooted in divine inspiration, crafted to stir spiritual awakening and strengthen faith. From intimate worship songs to high praise choruses, Segun’s music carries a unique sound from Heaven, anointed to heal and deliver.

Over the years, he has written and performed songs that resonate deeply with believers, creating an atmosphere of God’s presence wherever he ministers.

Live Ministrations

Segun Abon has ministered at various Celestial Church events, praise nights, revival gatherings, and interdenominational concerts. His stage presence is not for entertainment — it's a call to deeper worship and personal revival.

Vision

To awaken a new generation of worshippers through sound — igniting hearts for Christ and proclaiming the gospel through melody and lyrical truth. His mission is not just to sing, but to shift atmospheres and open heavens.

Songwriting & Albums

He is currently working on multiple projects and plans to release a full gospel album that captures testimonies, experiences, and spiritual journeys in music form.
